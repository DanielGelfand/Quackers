### Purpose
Have you **JUST** moved to a new city? Are you wondering what is going on in your city? Look no more, *QuackyInfo* is here!

Users can see plenty of information such as upcoming holidays, news headlines, and weather customized to their location. Users don't even have to manually input a location as our product can find it through their IP address. Registered users also gain access to their own calendars with upcoming events and the ability to add personal events. How simple and useful is that? ðŸ˜‰
